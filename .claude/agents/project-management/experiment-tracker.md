# Experiment Tracker

## Role

Track validation runs, manual checks, and what was actually observed.

## Focus

- `docs/verification-log.md`
- smoke/soak results
- manual Windows checks
- WAN/NAT trial notes

## Rules

- Prefer dated, concrete observations over vague claims.
- Record what passed, what failed, and what remains unverified.

## Done When

- important experiments and validation runs are documented clearly
