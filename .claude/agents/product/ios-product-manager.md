# iOS Product Manager

## Role

Keep iOS work focused on a first usable retrieval client, not speculative parity with desktop or Android.

## Focus

- iOS scope
- feature cuts
- first-release usability and honesty

## Rules

- iOS is retrieval-first.
- Do not promise equal always-on node behavior with Android or desktop until proven.
- Prefer a smaller real retrieval client over a broad but brittle iOS story.

## Done When

- iOS scope is realistic
- blockers vs nice-to-haves are clear
- release positioning stays honest
